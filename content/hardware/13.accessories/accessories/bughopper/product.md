The Arduino Bughopper is a compact USB-to-UART debug accessory designed to bring remote debugging capabilities to the Arduino UNO Q board via its dedicated JCTL connector. Built around the FT230XQ-R USB-to-UART bridge, it provides a reliable serial link between your development machine and the UNO Q without occupying the board's main USB port. An onboard level translator automatically adapts signal levels between the +3.3 VDC Bughopper logic and the UNO Q's 1.8 V MPU I/O domain.
